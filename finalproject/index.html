Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Some vegetarian diets can decrease the risk of heart disease, cancer, and type 2 diabetes.

  • They may lower your blod pressure.

  • Some vegetarian diets, especialy vegan, can decrease the symptoms of asthma.

  • Vegetarian diets can promote bone health and help prevent osteoporosis.

  • A vegetarian diet can help men to live up to 10 years longer, and women to live up to 6 years longer.

Cons:

  • If you aren't careful, a these restrictions can lead to nutrient deficiencies.

  • It can be inconvenient, especially when eating out.

  • Just because you are a vegetarian doesn't mean you are eating healthy, there are a lot of unhealthy vegetarian options.

  • Processed vegetarian foods often contain unhealthy amounts of fat, sugar, and sodium.
